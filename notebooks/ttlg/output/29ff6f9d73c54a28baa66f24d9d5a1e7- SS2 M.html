    There are two main types of engine - additive and subtractive.
    An additive engine (such as Quake) adds brushes to a void while a subtractive engine (Dark Engine, Unreal Engine) carve brushes out of an infinite solid. So yes, you would need a subtractive engine and a fair amount of programming skill.
