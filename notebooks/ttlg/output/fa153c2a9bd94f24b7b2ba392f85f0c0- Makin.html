    Hi. I haven't posted on any of the TTLG forums in close to a year (I think).

    I got on out of curiosity, seeing as how I just got back into SS2 (even a bit of multiplayer with a friend when he's online and we're both free).

    I made a new gamesys file that'd be perfect for your difficulty needs.
    It adds a couple of new items (accessible only by summon_obj command), but you don't need those unless you're *really* interested in a couple of fun toys to fidget with.
    The primary reason I made a new gamesys was to make multiplayer (and singleplayer for that matter) actually difficult. In singleplayer, Impossible difficulty is almost entirely impossible (for one of my meager skill). I increased all creature speeds by 25%. They move and fire 25% faster. Annelid worms and spiders are quirky in that their jumps suck now (due to their increased speed), but otherwise the game is much more challenging. The only thing you may not be interested in is the way I modified weapons. I felt that the high-end annelid and energy weapons were too weak, considering how late in the game you get them and how uncommon ammo is (at least on Impossible difficulty, that is). I boosted their damage by 25%. That means, of course, that you don't want to use a fusion cannon at point blank range. I also boosted shotgun damage by 25% (It felt extremely weak after you reach Hydroponics). Finally, I boosted the pistol's damage by 25% and lowered the assault rifle's damage by 25%. The reason being that they use the exact same ammunition, which means they should be relatively close in stopping power (The assault rifle is still more powerful because it has a longer barrel, adding accuracy and burning more powder while the bullet's still in it. I weakened the assault rifle a bit because I wanted the pistol's damage to be a bit closer than 60% or so of the assault rifle's, but I didn't want to overpower the pistol to an extreme level. At least that was my train of thought).

    I can't describe it very well beyond that.
    If you want a copy of it, just let me know how (E-mail or ICQ come to mind).
    If you don't want all the changes I made and instead want only the quicker enemies, I can make a fresh gamesys with just that change (I backed up the original, as anyone intelligent should).
    If you know how to use ShockEd and feel like using any idea presented here, kudos to you.

    I'm rambling. Somebody stop me.
