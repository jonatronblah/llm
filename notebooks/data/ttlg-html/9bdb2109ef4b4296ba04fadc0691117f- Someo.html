    Hi there:

    Ouch.

    I can't find a news report detailing the demise of LGS, but basically it's because the games weren't selling enough to keep them afloat and financially viable. Which is a huge shame.

    Fear not though - a developer in Texas going by the name of Ion Storm have taken over from LGS (hell, they have most of the ex-LG people anyway) and are continuing the LGS "legacy". Or Something<sup>TM</sup>.

    You should try the other games featured on this site - SS2, Deus Ex, Thief, etc... - as they all adher to the Looking Glass aesthetic, and you should like them.
